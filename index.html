    Opportunities to Improve Your Score

    To improve your mark for the semester, you may do any of the following multiple opportunities ...

    1. Turn in missing assignments with no point deduction for late work.
    2. Improve assignments turned in for an improved score.
    3. Enhance completed assignments for potential extra credit.

    Note: All students may improve their grades, not just students with failing grades.
